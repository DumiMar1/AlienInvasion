In Alien invasion, the player controls a rocket ship that appears at the bottom center of the screen. the player
can move the ship right or left using the arrow keys and shoot bullets using the spacebar. The game begins with a fleet
of aliens that fill the screen, and move across and down the screen. if you clear all the aliens by hitting them with th
e bullet, another have appears that moves faster. If you are touched or shot by the aliens, you lose a ship. If you lose
all 3 ships you lose.
